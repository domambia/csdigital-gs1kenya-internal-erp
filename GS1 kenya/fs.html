GS1 Barcode Beginner Course

Agenda:

  • This course is a must for all new members or those new to the role of managing product barcode numbers, providing attendees with a solid foundation for getting barcodes right; from the structuring of the code numbers to selecting the right symbol.

    On this course you will learn:

  • Overview Of The GS1 Company Systems
  • Identifying Products Using The Global Trade Item Number (GTIN)
  • Barcode Basics
  • Barcodes Allocations and Placement Guidelines
  • Barcodes Print Quality
